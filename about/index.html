Husband of a lovely wife, father of a beautiful boy and two cats, front-end developer by day, game developer by night.

The usual story. I always wanted to make games. The world convinced me that I couldn't do that. After several long years I became the President of Space and decided that nobody would prevent me from making games, so I started doing it.

I'm mainly interested in old-school retro pixel look and (especially) sound. I love old arcade games from the 70s and the 80s and that's what I'm going to be building while I gain my levels in gamemaking wizardry. Expect a lot of lasers, dragons, martians, dinosaurs, robots, ninjas and samurai. I'll try to stay away from zombies.

Also, none of this would have happened without the absolutely unbelievable and incredible support of my wife Anna who is the wind in my sails and whose encouragement was the force that got me to publish anything at all.
